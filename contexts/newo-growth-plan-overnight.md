## New Segments

### 1. Developers — "Phone Numbers for AI Apps"
**Insight:** Developers building AI apps (chatbots, agents, SaaS tools) need voice.
Twilio is expensive and complex. Newo can be the plug-and-play phone layer.
**Tagline:** "Add an AI voice to your app in 5 minutes."
**ICP:** Indie hackers, startup founders, AI developers, Claude/GPT app builders.
**Entry point:** `/add-newo` Claude skill (built ✅), `npx add-newo` installer.

### 2. Websites — "AI Receptionist for Any Website"
**Insight:** 95% of websites have no AI on their phone line. Any business with a
phone number on their website is a prospect. Newo can be the "add to cart" for
phone AI — trivial to install, instant value.
**Tagline:** "Turn your website phone number into an AI that never misses a call."
**ICP:** Any SMB with a website and a phone number (dentists, lawyers, accountants,
real estate agents, chiropractors, vets, contractors).
**Entry point:** One-line HTML snippet, like Google Analytics for phone.

### 3. AI Influencers — "Your Own AI for Fan Engagement"
**Insight:** Creators with large audiences want to "talk to" fans at scale. A phone
number powered by their personality + Newo = always-on fan engagement, 24/7.
Fans call/text and get an AI that sounds like and represents the creator.
**Tagline:** "Let your AI answer your fans — 24/7, in your voice."
**ICP:** YouTube creators, TikTokers, podcasters, newsletter writers, LinkedIn
influencers with 50K+ followers who monetize through courses, coaching, merch.
**Revenue model:** $299/month Creator plan. Influencer promotes Newo to audience.
Double play: influencer is a customer AND an affiliate.

### 4. Claude / AI Ecosystem — "AI Tool Stack Integration"
**Insight:** Claude Code users building web apps will encounter `/add-newo` at the
exact moment they're ready to wire up communication. Zero-friction organic discovery.
Position Newo as the official phone layer for AI-built applications.
**Tagline:** "The phone number infrastructure for AI-native apps."
**ICP:** Claude Code users, Cursor users, Replit builders, AI startup founders.
**Entry point:** Claude skill (built ✅), future: Anthropic partner directory.

## Key Decisions Made

1. **Developer segment uses the `/add-newo` Claude skill as primary acquisition channel.**
   Zero ad spend — capture developers exactly when they're building.

2. **Influencer segment is a double play:** influencer pays $299/month AND promotes Newo
   to their audience as an affiliate. Net cost to acquire each influencer: ~$0 if they
   promote organically.

3. **"Website" segment is the biggest total addressable market** but needs the simplest
   possible integration — one HTML tag, like a chat widget. Frame it as "Google Analytics
   for your phone line."

4. **Claude ecosystem is strategic long-term.** If Newo becomes the default phone layer
   for AI-built apps, it's defensible and doesn't require ad spend.
