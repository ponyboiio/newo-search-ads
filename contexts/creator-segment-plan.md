## The Opportunity

Creators with large audiences face a consistent problem: fans want access, but there's
only one of them. DMs are unmanageable at 100K+ followers. Email is dead. Their
assistant can't be on 24/7.

**Newo for Creators:** A phone number powered by your personality. Fans call or text.
Your AI answers — in your voice, with your content, your FAQs, your personality.

This is not customer support. This is fan engagement infrastructure.
