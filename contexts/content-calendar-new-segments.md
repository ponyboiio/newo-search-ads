## Production Notes

- All blog posts go to the main Newo blog — do NOT publish to a subdomain (SEO lesson)
- Each post should be 800–1,200 words minimum (Google favors depth)
- Include one internal link to a vertical page per post
- All posts should have a clear CTA button, not just a link
- LinkedIn posts perform best at 150–300 words with no external link in the body (put link in comments)
