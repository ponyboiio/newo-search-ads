## Competitive Intelligence Note

ServiceTitan already has a native **"AI Voice Agent"** product listed in their marketplace
("No More Missed Calls—AI Handles the Overflow"). This is direct competition for HVAC
contractors using ServiceTitan.

**Newo positioning against ST AI Voice Agent:**
- ST's is overflow-only ("handles overflow") — Newo answers ALL calls, not just overflow
- Newo works across ALL businesses, not just ST customers
- Newo has multi-channel (SMS, chat, Instagram, Yelp) — ST is phone only
- Newo has appointment booking with Google Calendar/Calendly — check if ST has this
- For non-ST contractors: Newo is the only option

For outreach to HVAC contacts, lead with: "Unlike ServiceTitan's built-in voice tool,
Newo works for any contractor — not just ST users — and handles every channel, not just calls."
